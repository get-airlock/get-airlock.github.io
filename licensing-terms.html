Licensing Framework: Financial Services Vertical

Proposed structure for an exclusive commercial partnership between Airlock Labs and TraderVerse.
The deal in one sentence: TraderVerse becomes the exclusive reseller of the Airlock Router for financial services software in exchange for an upfront fee, an annual minimum, and a 30% revenue share. Airlock keeps all IP and everything outside the defined scope.

1. Scope of License

CategoryDefinition
In-scope (exclusive)Financial services software products: trading platforms, brokerages, fintech apps, crypto exchanges, portfolio managers, wealth management tools, financial advisors (human-AI hybrid), and financial education platforms.
Out-of-scope (retained)Enterprise direct, academic, research partnerships, open source distribution, content creators, gaming, healthcare, government, defense, agriculture, manufacturing, and any vertical not explicitly named above.
Right of first refusalIf an in-scope customer approaches Airlock directly, TraderVerse has 30 days to match terms or the deal passes to Airlock.

2. Financial Structure

TermAmountNotes
Upfront license fee$250,000Paid on signing. Non-refundable. Funds Airlock operations.
Annual minimum royalty$500,000Paid quarterly ($125K/quarter). Counts against revenue share.
Revenue share30% to AirlockOf gross revenue from sublicense, resale, or in-product use.
Performance clause$2M bookings by month 18If not hit, exclusivity terminates, license becomes non-exclusive at same terms.
Exclusivity period18 monthsAuto-renews for 12 months if annual minimum met and performance clause satisfied.

3. Rights

Airlock retains

  • All IP: router, benchmark, DECF framework, scoring engine, hooks architecture
  • Research direction and publication rights
  • Airlock brand, website, GitHub, HuggingFace, social accounts
  • Right to build any non-financial product without notice
  • Right to publish open source versions of non-commercial components
  • Right to accept grants, research funding, and academic partnerships
  • 100% of company equity. No board seats granted.

TraderVerse receives

  • Exclusive right to resell Airlock Router into financial services vertical
  • Co-branded marketing and sales collateral
  • Priority on feature requests for financial use cases
  • 30-day advance access to new research and releases
  • White-label option for end-customer deployments
  • Dedicated technical contact at Airlock for integration support
  • Joint press and case study rights

4. Termination and Exit

  • 90-day notice for material breach by either party.
  • Acquisition clause: If TraderVerse is acquired, the license terminates unless Airlock approves the acquirer in writing.
  • Missed minimums: Two consecutive quarters below minimum royalty terminates exclusivity automatically.
  • Succession: If Zachary Holwerda becomes incapacitated, IP transfers to a designated successor and the license continues under the same terms.
  • Post-termination: TraderVerse retains right to service existing customers under prior terms for 12 months, but cannot sign new deals.
