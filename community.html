01 What the Spacebar is

The Spacebar is the operational shell of the Airlock substrate. It's a fork of Spacebar Chat — fully Discord-compatible, fully self-hostable — wrapped in a Tauri native desktop app and served as the operator's command center.

Channels, threads, voice, file storage, push notifications: everything Discord does, running on your machine. Plus the substrate underneath that Discord doesn't have — bivector-closed audit logs, persona-aware routing, the Maybe Protocol gate, the eight Octo arms, the OfficeCLI staff archetypes producing operational packets in the background.

The community using the Spacebar is the org tree of the team running it. Operators above; substrate below; staff archetypes routing packets between them.
